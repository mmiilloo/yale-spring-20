PUBLIC AXIS is a collective of makers, thinkers and communicators centered in New Haven, CT. Our mission is to help community organizers and activists amplify their voice in any way we can. In the name of social, environmental and racial justice, we offer free consultation, graphic design, translating services, print layout for flyers, pamphlets, books and posters, web and interactive design, video editing, animation, outreach and are happy to share knowledge and resources.
